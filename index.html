• ABOUT

    Sonic Megamix Mania is a non-profit modification of 'Sonic Mania' that both faithfully recreates the levels from the late 'Sonic Megamix' (created by Stealth) and even re-imagines them in a new mode named after the original ROM hack! Current plans for the next release include a port to the Hatch Engine, hyper forms and much more!
